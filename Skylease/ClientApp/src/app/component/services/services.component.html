Perishable

SkyLease Cargo is the No. 1 cargo carrier in the transportation and handling of perishable goods to and from Miami International Airport. Half of SkyLease cargo is perishable: KYE carries fresh flowers from Colombia , fruits and asparagus from Peru, Fresh salmon & stone fruits / berries from Chile and other products that require temperature control, offering a cooler of 15,000 m2 making it a leader in perishable handling and cold chain services in the continent.
Our Warehouse is Certified by The United States Environmental Protection Agency for the use of refrigerants and residues of our cooling systems in the category of (Refrigerated Warehousing and Storage). SkyLease Cargo is also a proud member of Flora Life® a well recognized organization that provides support and knowledge of handling and transportation of perishables products. From Growers to customers and to retail stores.

KYE have the knowledge, experience and capacity to ship all your oversized or heavy cargo – any size, anytime, anyplace. KYE BIG & Heavy is our solution for your extremely large, heavy or atypical cargo shipments.

Dangerous Goods

Solids, liquids, or gases that can harm people, other living organisms, property, or the environment, are often subject to chemical regulations. In the United States and other countries, dangerous goods are more commonly known as hazardous materials, (HAZMAT or HazMat).
KYE have specialized personnel to handle in a safe, secure, responsible and diligent manner.
The primary features of this service are:
- Boarding priority
- Specialized handling by trained and highly qualified personnel
- Storage in specialized areas.
- Our Freighter fleet allows us to handle a wider variety of dangerous goods.
WE Care is the transportation solution to all kind of Dangerous Goods

Sensitive and Valuable Cargo

KYE guarantee higher priority and safe transportation of goods that due to it´s nature require expedite handling and special security measures. Items such as: gold, bank notes, high value metals, jewelry, high-tech electronics, to name a few, are escorted by our security staff at origin, transit and destination points.
Contingency plans are always in place, in case of flight delays, alternative airport landings, and other issues which may crop up during transportation.
Valuable Cargo is kept under constant surveillance during the entire process, from the moment of receipt from the shipper, until delivered to the consignee. KYE uses specially-secured areas for this cargo. There is constant communication between all personnel involved in each shipment´s handling. This heightened coordination between our security and operational teams guarantees the integrity of each shipment at all times.
