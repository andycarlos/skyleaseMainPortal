About Company


SkyLease Cargo is the leader in full freighter service provider from its US headquarter in Miami with strong presence in the market for more than 25 years. SkyLease is a US well established cargo airline operating scheduled flights in the Americas and Asia , additionally KYE operate charters worldwide.


SkyLease focuses on providing efficient air freight services with a strong emphasis in customer service. KYE ensure quick responses to our customers changing requirements offering a broad array of services based our MD-11’s and B747-400- ERF fleet. In 2013, Centurion / SkyLease Cargo moved to its brand new, state-of-the -art facility situated in a privileged position the Miami International Airport. The facility comprises a 130,000 m² area incorporating warehouse with both cold and dry storage facilities, and 92 cargo docks.
